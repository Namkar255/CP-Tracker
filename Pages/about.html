About CP Tracker

CP Tracker is a platform designed to help competitive
programmers track their progress, manage goals, stay updated
with contests, and discover useful learning resources.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a single platform where programmers can monitor their growth, organize their learning journey, and stay motivated while preparing for coding interviews and programming contests.

What CP Tracker Offers

Dashboard

Monitor your coding progress and achievements.

Goals

Set and track personal coding milestones.

Contests

Stay informed about upcoming programming contests.

Resources

Access curated learning materials and practice sheets.
